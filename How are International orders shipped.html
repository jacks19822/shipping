How are International orders shipped?

International shipments are shipped via FedEx International Economy. This method takes between 3 - 7 days for delivery.

FedEx International Priority is also available and takes between 2 - 5 days for delivery.

We accept EFT (Electronic Funds Transfer) for all overseas shipments (Canada and UK - we accept EFT or Credit Card payment). Once transfer is complete, the product ships immediately.
