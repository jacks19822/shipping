Can I refuse a shipment?

We ask that you follow our return policy for faster processing as refused shipments may take up to 3 weeks to be returned to our warehouse causing a delay in processing your credit.

We do incur shipping charges on all shipments to our customers. If there is an error on our part, these shipping fees will be refunded or credited along with the cost of the item(s) purchased. If through no fault of AtBatt.com the shipment is refused (or returned), shipping charges will not be credited and you may be charged a 15% restocking fee.
