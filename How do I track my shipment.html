How do I track my shipment?

Once an order ships from our warehouse, you will be able to track your shipment. Click on "Order Status" under the Information Desk on the left side of your screen. Enter your order number and click on "Get Orders". Once your order comes up, you will see the status of your order along with the item(s) ordered, pricing, quantity and totals.

Under Shipping / Tracking Information you will see the tracking number that was assigned to your order.

UPS tracking numbers are usually 18 digits long (1ZA8XXXXXXXXXXXXXX ). Go to http://www.ups.com/tracking/tracking.html and enter the tracking number in one of the fields provided then click on "Track". The tracking number will be displayed. If you need detailed information, click on "Details".

FedEx Ground tracking numbers are usually 12 digits long ( 6XXXXXXXXXXX ). Go to http://www.fedex.com/ and enter the tracking number in the field provided and click on "Track It". The detailed results of the tracking number you entered will be displayed.
